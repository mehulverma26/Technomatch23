Our weather predicting is developed on Intel API- DAL. It shows data of OAKLAND INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT, CA, US for the years from 1960- 2022. The Intel oneAPI Data Analytics Library (oneDAL) contributes to the acceleration of big data analysis by providing highly optimized algorithmic building blocks for all phases of data analytics (preprocessing, transformation, analysis, modelling, validation, and decision making) in batch, online, and distributed processing modes of computation
